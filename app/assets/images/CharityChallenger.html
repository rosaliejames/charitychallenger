Sign Up

Signing up with CharityChallenger is quick and easy. Just enter your email, pick a password, and you're ready to start achieving your goals!

Challenge Your Friends

After you've signed up, you can challenge another user. Select a user from our drop down menu and enter a challenge and charity of your choice.

Help Others

When you create a challenge, you donate a dollar for each day of the challenge. Every day that you complete your challenge, the dollar goes to your charity; when you don't, it goes to your challenger's charity.

Accomplish Your Goals

By providing two forms of external motivation to hold you accountable - helping others and friendly competition - CharityChallenger helps you achieve your goals!
